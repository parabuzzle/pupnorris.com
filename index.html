Pup Norris




They Call Me Pup Norris!



I'm a rescued pit bull puppy from Pit Crew Rescue in Sacramento. I was found as a puppy with all my siblings and my mommy. We were all locked in a small crate and weeks went by without any food or water but my momma looked out for all of us through those tough times until the nice lady from Pit Crew Rescue came and saved us all.

I was adopted on February 20th, 2016 (which was also National Love Your Pet Day), and things have been going great! My new forever home is great and has a yard and they even have a furry cat named Leo. He pretty much runs the place but that's ok because he let's me play with him. I love playing with him so much! He doesn't seem to like it much but we're working on it.

They also have me in training to be a service dog. I love helping out and when I'm a certified service dog I'll have the greatest job in the world, helping my new momma! (I'm a little excited about it)

Thanks for stopping by and make sure you follow me on social media because I love taking photos and videos of my adventures (and I love all the awesome virtual love I get... I am a dog after all)
